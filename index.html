1. Introduction

This web app is designed to help users manage their food inventory and maintain a healthy lifestyle by offering meal plans based on scanned food items and suggesting exercises tailored to their dietary intake and preferences.

The purpose of this app is to assist users in making informed dietary choices and encourage regular physical activity to promote overall health and wellness.

  • Health-conscious individuals
  • Busy professionals
  • Families looking for meal planning solutions
  • Fitness enthusiasts

2. Features

Description: Users can scan food items in their fridge or kitchen using their device's camera.

Functionality:

  • Use Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to identify food items.
  • Maintain a database of common food items for accurate identification.

Description: Generates daily and weekly meal plans based on the available scanned food items.

Functionality:

  • Use a recipe database to match ingredients with available items.
  • Offer meal suggestions considering dietary preferences (vegan, vegetarian, etc.).
  • Nutritional analysis for each meal suggested.

  • Use Google Maps API to find and display routes.

3. Technical Architecture

Technologies: React.js, HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Responsiveness: Mobile-first design to accommodate scanning on mobile devices.

State Management: Redux for managing application state.

Technologies: Node.js, Express.js

APIs: RESTful API for communication between frontend and backend.

Security: JWT for user authentication.

Technologies: MongoDB or PostgreSQL

Schema:

  • Users: Store user profile, height, weight, and preferences.
  • Food Items: Store scanned food items and their nutritional information.
  • Recipes: Store meal recipes associated with ingredients.

Food Scanning: OCR API (e.g., Tesseract.js or Google Vision API).

Exercise Data: Use a fitness API for exercise recommendations.

Google Maps: Google Maps Directions API for routing.

4. Usage Instructions

  • Open the app and click on the "Scan" button.
  • Point your camera at the food item and click to capture.
  • The app will process the image and display identified items.
  • Navigate to the meal planning section after scanning.
  • View suggested meals based on scanned items.
  • Select meals to save them for the week.
  • View exercise recommendations based on your scanned meal intake.
  • Adjust settings to personalize the type of exercise suggested.
  • Navigate to the exercise suggestions section.
  • Select "View Routes" to see nearby routes based on your preferences.
  • Choose a route to view details and start navigation.

5. Future Enhancements

6. Conclusion

This web app aims to promote healthy eating and exercise by providing a comprehensive solution that combines food scanning, meal planning, exercise recommendations, and Google Maps integration. By addressing the needs of health-conscious users, this app strives to foster a healthier lifestyle.
